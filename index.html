About me

In 2018 I started my PhD at Université Sorbonne Paris Nord under the supervision of Farrell Brumley. Since 2021 I am simultaneously a visitor at Universität Bonn.

My mathematical interests are mostly supported on analytic number theory, automorphic forms and representation theory.
